Our Platform

Meet Orcheo

Orcheo is our proprietary AI workflow orchestration platform built on LangGraph. It enables rapid development and deployment of sophisticated AI systems with a node-based architecture.

  • Visual workflow builder for complex AI pipelines
  • Pre-built integrations with OpenAI, Slack, Telegram, MongoDB
  • Real-time monitoring and WebSocket support
  • Extensible node system for custom integrations
  • Both low-code and code-first development options
